# 💎 Jewelry Business Intelligence (BI) Dashboard
### Analyze Sales, Inventory, and Financial Performance

---

## 🇺🇸 English Version

### 📊 Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive **Business Intelligence solution** built for a jewelry retail business. It centralizes sales, purchasing, and expense data to provide clear insights into profitability and inventory management. The dashboard enables business owners to move from "gut feeling" to data-driven decision-making.

### 🚀 Key Features
*   **Financial Health Tracking:** Monitoring Revenue, Gross Profit, and Net Margin in real-time.
*   **Inventory Control:** Tracking gold stock levels (in grams) to prevent stockouts or overstocking.
*   **Expense Analysis:** Categorization of operational costs (Rent, Salaries, Logistics) to optimize spending.
*   **Trend Identification:** Visualizing sales peaks and seasonal trends over days and months.
*   **Vendor Performance:** Analyzing which factories/suppliers offer the most profitable products.

### 🛠 Tech Stack
*   **Power BI:** Data visualization and report authoring.
*   **Power Query:** ETL process (Cleaning and transforming raw data).
*   **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Advanced measures for ROI, stock balance, and profit margins.
*   **Data Source:** Excel/Google Sheets.

### 🚀 Asosiy imkoniyatlar
*   **Moliyaviy ko'rsatkichlar:** Umumiy tushum, yalpi foyda va sof foyda ko'rsatkichlarini real vaqt rejimida kuzatish.
*   **Inventar nazorati:** Oltin qoldig'ini (grammda) nazorat qilish va doimiy zaxirani ta'minlash.
*   **Xarajatlar tahlili:** Operatsion xarajatlarni (ijara, oyliklar, logistika) kategoriyalarga bo'lib tahlil qilish.
*   **Trendlarni aniqlash:** Savdo hajmining o'sish va pasayish davrlarini kunlik va oylik kesimda ko'rish.
*   **Hamkorlar tahlili:** Qaysi zavodlar yoki yetkazib beruvchilar eng daromadli mahsulotlarni taqdim etishini aniqlash.

### 🛠 Texnologiyalar
*   **Power BI:** Vizualizatsiya va hisobotlar yaratish.
*   **Power Query:** Ma'lumotlarni tozalash va formatlash (ETL).

*   **Ma'lumot manbai:** Excel/Google Sheets.
